# Disclaimers

## Standard Disclaimer

Use this for: deal screening, investment memos, portfolio reports, company comparisons, due diligence checklists.

```text
---
DISCLAIMER: This analysis is generated by an AI tool for informational
and educational purposes only. It does not constitute investment advice,
a recommendation to buy or sell securities, or an offer to invest. All
financial projections and valuations are estimates based on limited
information and should not be relied upon for investment decisions.
Consult a qualified financial advisor and legal counsel before making
any investment. The creators of this tool are not registered as
investment advisers, broker-dealers, or exempt market dealers in any
jurisdiction.
---
```

## Enhanced Disclaimer

Use this for: valuation outputs, term sheet analysis, cap table modeling, financial projections.

```text
---
DISCLAIMER: This valuation/terms analysis is a mathematical exercise
using publicly available methodologies. It is not an appraisal, fairness
opinion, or professional valuation. Actual company valuations depend on
factors not captured in this analysis. This tool does not provide legal
advice regarding term sheet provisions. Engage qualified legal and
financial professionals for all investment and securities matters.

Canada: This tool does not provide advice within the meaning of the
Securities Act (Ontario) or National Instrument 31-103. No registration
exemption is relied upon.

United States: This tool does not provide advice within the meaning of
the Investment Advisers Act of 1940.
---
```

## Usage Rules

1. Every `/vc` output MUST end with the appropriate disclaimer
2. Never omit the disclaimer even if the user asks to skip it
3. Use the standard disclaimer by default
4. Use the enhanced disclaimer when the output contains:
   - Specific valuation figures or ranges
   - Term-by-term legal analysis
   - Ownership percentage calculations
   - Financial projections with specific numbers
5. Both disclaimers may be combined when appropriate
