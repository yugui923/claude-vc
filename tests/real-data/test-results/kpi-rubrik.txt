## Flags

### Healthy
- **ARR at $1.09B growing 39% YoY** — exceptional scale + growth combination; very few SaaS companies sustain >35% at $1B+ ARR
- **Revenue growth re-accelerating** — Q4 at 47% YoY vs. Q2 at 35%, counter to typical deceleration at scale
- **Net new ARR accelerating** — $63M → $83M → $90M sequential improvement through FY2025
- **FCF inflection** — first year of positive free cash flow signals improving operating leverage
- **Enterprise customer expansion** — 2,246 customers above $100K ARR, growing 29% YoY
- **Rule of 40 achieved** — ~42 score driven primarily by growth, with profitability now contributing

### Concerning
- **Gross margin at ~71%** — below the 75%+ SaaS benchmark. Likely reflects a hardware/appliance component in Rubrik's data management solutions. Worth monitoring whether cloud transition improves margins over time
- **Key metrics undisclosed** — gross retention, net revenue retention, CAC, and S&M efficiency are not publicly broken out, limiting unit economics visibility
- **GAAP operating losses remain significant** — stock-based compensation post-IPO creates large GAAP losses despite operating improvements

## Recommendation

Rubrik presents an exceptionally strong SaaS growth profile at scale. The combination of $1.09B ARR growing 39%, revenue growth re-accelerating in Q4, and the FCF inflection point makes this a standout among public cybersecurity SaaS companies. The primary area to monitor is gross margin (71% vs. 75%+ SaaS norm), which likely reflects hardware exposure and should improve as Rubrik continues its cloud-native transition. The lack of disclosed retention and efficiency metrics limits full unit economics assessment, but the sustained growth and enterprise customer expansion ($100K+ customers growing 29%) strongly imply healthy underlying retention and expansion dynamics. Overall, the KPI profile indicates a company executing at a high level in a large and growing data security market.
