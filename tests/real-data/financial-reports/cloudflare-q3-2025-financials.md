# Cloudflare (NET) - Q3 2025 Financial Results
## Source: Official Earnings Press Release, November 2025

### Company Overview
Cloudflare is a global cloud services provider offering a connectivity cloud platform
with security, performance, and developer services. SaaS business model with
usage-based and subscription pricing.

### Q3 2025 (Quarter ended September 30, 2025)

**Revenue:**
- Total revenue: $562.0 million (31% YoY growth)

**Profitability:**
- GAAP gross profit: $415.7 million (74.0% margin)
- Non-GAAP gross profit: $423.1 million (75.3% margin)
- GAAP operating loss: $(37.5) million (6.7% of revenue)
- Non-GAAP operating income: $85.9 million (15.3% of revenue)
- GAAP net loss: $(1.3) million
- Non-GAAP net income: $102.6 million

**Per Share:**
- Non-GAAP EPS: $0.27

**Cash Flow:**
- Operating cash flow: $167.1 million
- Free cash flow: $75.0 million (13% of revenue)
- Cash and equivalents: $4,043.5 million

**Growth Metrics:**
- RPO growth: 43% YoY
- Current RPO growth: 30% YoY

### Full Year 2025 Guidance
- Revenue: $2,142.0–$2,143.0 million (~30% YoY growth)

- FY2025 Non-GAAP EPS: $0.91
