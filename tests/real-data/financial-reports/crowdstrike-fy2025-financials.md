# CrowdStrike Holdings (CRWD) - FY2025 Financial Results
## Source: Official Earnings Press Release, March 4, 2025

### Company Overview
CrowdStrike is a cybersecurity SaaS company providing cloud-delivered endpoint security,
cloud workload protection, identity security, and next-generation SIEM.

**Revenue:**
- Total revenue: $1.058 billion (25% YoY growth)
- Subscription revenue: $1.008 billion (27% YoY growth)
- Professional services revenue: $50.0 million

**ARR:**
