### The Churn Story — 11% Monthly, But Read the Fine Print
- February monthly churn: 11.0% (vs 4.2% trailing average)
- Gross churned MRR: $63,100

**Breakdown of churn:**
- Completion-driven churn: $38,200 (60.5% of total churn)
  - 420 learners completed their full learning path and graduated
  - These users had an average NPS of 78 and 94% course completion
  - 31% have already referred colleagues — referral pipeline is $180K
- Normal attrition: $14,800 (23.5%)
- Budget cuts at 2 mid-market clients: $10,100 (16.0%)

**Why completion churn is a product success signal:**
Our trade union partnerships (IBEW, UA) fund 6-month upskilling cohorts. When
learners complete, the contract ends — but unions re-up for next cohort 85% of
the time. The "churn" is structural to the cohort model, not dissatisfaction.
Adjusted for cohort completion, underlying churn is 3.8%.

- Total views: 4.2M
- Inbound leads generated: 3,400 (at $0.82 CPL vs $8.50 on LinkedIn)
- Lead-to-trial conversion: 12% (408 trials started)
- Trial-to-paid conversion (estimated): 22% based on early cohort data
- Projected new MRR from campaign: $45,000-$60,000 over next 60 days
- Risk: TikTok-sourced users have 15% lower average contract value — more individual
  learners, fewer enterprise seats
