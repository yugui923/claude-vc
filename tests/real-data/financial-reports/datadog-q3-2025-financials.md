# Datadog (DDOG) - Q3 2025 Financial Results
## Source: Official Earnings Press Release

### Company Overview
Datadog is a monitoring and analytics SaaS platform for cloud-scale applications.
The company provides infrastructure monitoring, APM, log management, security,
and AI observability through a unified platform.

### Q3 2025 (Quarter ended September 30, 2025)

**Revenue:**
- Total revenue: $886 million (28% YoY growth)
- Nine months 2025 revenue: $2,474 million (vs $1,947M in 2024)

**Profitability:**
- GAAP operating loss: $(6) million; Margin: (1)%
- Non-GAAP operating income: $207 million; Margin: 23%
- GAAP net income: $33.9 million
- Non-GAAP net income: $197.4 million

**Per Share:**
- GAAP diluted EPS: $0.10
- Non-GAAP diluted EPS: $0.55

**Cash Flow:**
- Operating cash flow: $251 million
- Free cash flow: $214 million
- Nine months free cash flow: $623.7 million

**Balance Sheet:**
- Cash and equivalents: $540.6 million
- Marketable securities: $3,599.7 million
- Total liquid assets: ~$4.1 billion

### Customer Metrics
- $100K+ ARR customers: 4,060 (up 16% from 3,490 YoY)
- Platform integrations: 1,000+
- AI native customers: 12% of revenue (up from 6% YoY)
- 15 AI native customers spending $1M+ annually
- ~100 AI native customers spending $100K+ annually

### FY2025 Guidance
- Full year revenue: $3,386–$3,390 million
- Non-GAAP operating income: $754–$758 million
- Non-GAAP EPS: $2.00–$2.02
