# Rubrik (RBRK) - FY2025 Financial Results
## Source: Official Earnings Press Release, March 2025

### Company Overview
Rubrik is a data security and management SaaS company providing backup,
recovery, and cyber resilience solutions. IPO'd April 2024 on NYSE at $32/share.
Company type: SaaS / Cybersecurity / Data Management.

### Q4 FY2025 (Quarter ended January 31, 2025)

**Revenue:**
- Total revenue: $258.1 million (47% YoY growth)

**ARR:**
- Subscription ARR: $1,092.6 million (39% YoY growth)

**Customer Metrics:**
- Customers with $100K+ ARR: 2,246 (up 29% YoY)

### Full Year FY2025

**Revenue:**
- Total revenue: $886.5 million (41% YoY growth)
- Subscription revenue: $828.7 million (54% YoY growth)
