# GreenGrid Exchange — FY2025 Annual Financial Report
## Period: January 1 – December 31, 2025
## Company Type: Climate Tech (Carbon Credit Marketplace)

### Revenue Summary
- Gross Merchandise Volume (GMV): $6,200,000
- Net Revenue (marketplace take): $1,800,000
- Blended Take Rate: 29.0%
- Take Rate Decline: from 32.1% (FY2024) to 29.0% (FY2025)
- YoY GMV Growth: 88%
- YoY Net Revenue Growth: 64%

### Take Rate Erosion — Key Trend
| Customer Tier | GMV | Take Rate | Net Revenue |
|--------------|-----|-----------|-------------|
| Enterprise (>$500K GMV) | $2,480,000 (40%) | 24.2% | $600,160 |
| Mid-Market ($100K-$500K) | $2,170,000 (35%) | 29.5% | $640,150 |
| SMB (<$100K) | $1,550,000 (25%) | 36.1% | $559,550 |
| Total | $6,200,000 | 29.0% | $1,799,860 |

Enterprise clients grew from 28% to 40% of GMV in FY2025, pulling blended take
rate down. Three enterprise clients negotiated volume discounts below 25%. If
enterprise mix reaches 50% (projected FY2026), blended take rate could drop to 27%.

**Q4 Concentration Note:** Q4 represented 61% of annual GMV (target was 45%).
Corporate ESG reporting deadlines and fiscal year-end budget flush drive massive
Q4 demand. This creates operational challenges — verification capacity maxes out
in Nov/Dec, causing 3-5 day delays vs <24 hours in Q1-Q3.
