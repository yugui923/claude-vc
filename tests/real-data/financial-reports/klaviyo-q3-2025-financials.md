# Klaviyo (KVYO) - Q3 2025 Financial Results
## Source: Official Earnings Press Release, November 5, 2025

### Company Overview
Klaviyo is a B2C CRM and marketing automation SaaS platform powering email,
SMS, push notifications, and reviews for e-commerce brands. IPO'd in 2023.
Company type: SaaS / Marketing Technology.

### Q3 2025 (Quarter ended September 30, 2025)

**Revenue:**
- Total revenue: $311 million (32% YoY growth)
- International revenue growth: 43% YoY (EMEA and APAC)

**Profitability:**
- Non-GAAP operating income: ~$43.5–$46.5 million (estimated from guidance pattern)
- Non-GAAP operating margin: ~14%

### Customer Metrics

- Customers with $50K+ ARR: 3,563 (up 36% from 2,619 YoY)
- Net revenue retention (NRR): 109%
