## Slide 2: The Crisis
- Physicians spend 2 hours on documentation for every 1 hour of patient care (AMA 2025 survey)
- Clinical burnout has reached 63% — documentation burden is the #1 cited cause
- Medical scribes cost $36-45/hr and have 78% annual turnover
- EHR documentation errors contribute to 250,000 adverse events annually (Johns Hopkins)
- Health systems lose $150K+ per physician annually to documentation overhead and burnout-driven attrition

---

## Slide 3: Solution
**MedScribe AI** is an ambient AI scribe that listens to patient encounters and generates:
- SOAP notes, H&P documentation, and procedure notes in real-time
- Structured data fields for EHR auto-population (Epic, Cerner, Meditech, athenahealth)
- ICD-10 and CPT coding suggestions with 96.7% accuracy
- Patient-facing visit summaries in plain language (12 languages)
- Clinical decision support alerts surfaced during documentation

## Slide 6: Product Deep Dive
- **Ambient Capture**: Always-on microphone with HIPAA-compliant on-device processing — no audio leaves the exam room
- **SmartNote Engine**: Generates specialty-specific notes (deployed across 23 medical specialties)
- **EHR Sync**: Bi-directional integration with Epic (certified), Cerner (certified), athenahealth, Meditech
- **Coding Copilot**: Suggests ICD-10 and CPT codes; has captured $18.4M in previously missed reimbursement for clients
- **Quality Module**: Flags documentation gaps that affect quality measures (HEDIS, MIPS)
- **PENDING**: AI diagnostic suggestion feature awaiting FDA 510(k) clearance (submitted August 2025, expected decision Q3 2026). This feature is NOT included in current revenue projections
