# TalentBridge - Series A Pitch Deck
## The AI Marketplace for Fractional Engineering Talent

---

## Slide 1: Title
**TalentBridge** — AI-Powered Marketplace for Fractional Engineering Teams
Series A Fundraise: $12M at $45M pre-money valuation

---

## Slide 2: Problem
- 71% of startups can't hire senior engineers fast enough (avg time-to-hire: 67 days)
- $150B+ spent annually on software outsourcing, mostly with poor quality outcomes
- Freelance marketplaces (Toptal, Upwork) lack vetting and team coordination
- Companies need fractional senior talent, not full-time hires they can't afford

---

## Slide 3: Solution
**TalentBridge** matches startups with vetted, fractional engineering squads:
- AI-powered matching: skills, timezone, culture, domain expertise
- Pre-vetted talent: only top 3% pass our technical + soft skill assessment
- Squad model: coordinated 2-4 person teams, not individual freelancers
- Built-in project management, code review, and delivery tracking

---

## Slide 4: Why Now
- Remote work normalized fractional/distributed teams permanently
- AI can now assess developer quality with 92% accuracy (vs 60% for humans)
- Startup funding pulled back → companies need flexible talent, not FTEs
- Developer supply outpacing demand in many regions (LatAm, Eastern Europe, SEA)

---

## Slide 5: Market Size
- **TAM**: $430B — Global IT staffing and outsourcing market
- **SAM**: $85B — Software engineering staffing (freelance + contract)
- **SOM**: $4.2B — Fractional senior engineering teams for startups/SMBs
- Market growing at 18% CAGR

---

## Slide 6: Product
- **AI Matching Engine**: 47 signals to match teams to projects
- **TalentScore**: Proprietary vetting system (coding, system design, communication)
- **Squad Builder**: Auto-compose balanced teams (lead, senior, mid-level)
- **Delivery Dashboard**: Track velocity, code quality, standup summaries
- **PayGuard**: Milestone-based escrow payments with dispute resolution

---

## Slide 7: Business Model
- **Marketplace model** — 25% take rate on talent billings
- Average squad: 3 engineers × $85/hr × 120 hrs/mo = $30,600/mo GMV
- Average take rate revenue: $7,650/mo per active squad
- Annual revenue per squad: $91,800
- Gross margin: 72% (after matching costs, platform ops, payment processing)
