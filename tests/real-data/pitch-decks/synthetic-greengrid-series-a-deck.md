## Slide 2: Problem
- $2B+ in carbon credits have been found to be worthless or double-counted (Guardian/Die Zeit investigation, 2024)
- Corporates have committed $180B in net-zero pledges but lack credible offset procurement channels
- Verification takes 12-18 months and costs $50K-200K per project — blocking small and mid-size developers
- Verra and Gold Standard registries are opaque, slow, and paper-heavy
- Buyers can't differentiate quality: a $3/ton credit and a $45/ton credit look identical on paper
- Result: corporate buyers are paralyzed. 62% have frozen voluntary offset purchases pending better quality assurance (McKinsey Carbon Survey 2025)

---

## Slide 3: Solution
**GreenGrid** is a marketplace for verified carbon credits with end-to-end quality assurance:
- **AI-Powered Verification**: Satellite imagery + ML models verify project additionality and permanence in weeks, not months
- **Quality Score**: Proprietary 0-100 rating for every credit, combining 38 data signals
- **Marketplace**: Corporates browse, compare, and purchase verified credits with full provenance
- **Retirement Registry**: Blockchain-anchored retirement tracking eliminates double-counting
- **Monitoring**: Continuous satellite monitoring of active projects (not just at issuance)

---

## Slide 4: Why Now
- EU Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM) taking effect 2026 — corporates must account for embedded carbon
- SEC climate disclosure rules finalized in 2025 — offset quality now a board-level concern
- Satellite resolution hit 30cm in commercial imagery — enables plot-level forest verification
- Voluntary carbon market contracted 18% in 2024 due to trust crisis — the shakeout is clearing space for quality-focused platforms
- Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi) updated guidance in 2025 to allow high-quality offsets in Scope 3 — this reopened the market

## Slide 6: Product
- **GreenGrid Verify**: Upload project data, satellite coordinates, and documentation. AI assessment delivered in 3-6 weeks (vs. 12-18 months traditional)
- **GreenGrid Score**: 0-100 quality rating based on: additionality (25%), permanence (25%), measurement rigor (20%), co-benefits (15%), leakage risk (15%)
- **GreenGrid Market**: Order book for buyers and sellers. Credits listed by project type, geography, vintage, quality score
- **GreenGrid Track**: Blockchain retirement registry integrated with Verra, Gold Standard, and ACR
- **GreenGrid Monitor**: Quarterly satellite imagery review of all active forestry and land-use projects
- Project types supported: Forestry/REDD+, renewable energy, methane capture, direct air capture, soil carbon, blue carbon

## Slide 13: Risks and Honest Assessment
- **Market risk**: VCM could continue contracting. Our counter-thesis: quality-verified credits are counter-cyclical within the market — as junk credits get purged, verified credits gain share. But we acknowledge this thesis is unproven at scale
- **Regulatory uncertainty**: Carbon credit frameworks vary by jurisdiction and are evolving. Article 6 of the Paris Agreement is still being implemented. Changes could help or hurt
- **Verification accuracy**: Our AI models have 91% agreement with human auditors on quality scoring. That 9% gap matters — one high-profile false positive could damage trust. We maintain human-in-the-loop for all projects scoring 40-60 (the gray zone)
- **Valuation justification**: 40x revenue is high. We believe this is justified by (a) 343% revenue growth, (b) $45M verification pipeline, and (c) regulatory tailwinds. But we're transparent that this multiple assumes continued market expansion
- **Carbon price volatility**: EU ETS and voluntary market prices can swing 30%+ in a quarter. We don't take principal risk (marketplace model) but our GMV is directly exposed
