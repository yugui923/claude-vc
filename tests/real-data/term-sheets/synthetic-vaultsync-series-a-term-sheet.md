# CONVERTIBLE NOTE TERM SHEET
## VaultSync, Inc. — Series A Bridge Financing

**Date:** February 10, 2026
**Confidential**

This Term Sheet summarizes the proposed terms of a convertible promissory note
offering by VaultSync, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the "Company"). This
document is non-binding except for "No-Shop," "Confidentiality," and "Expenses."

---

### NOTE TERMS

**Maturity Date:** 18 months from the initial closing date (September 15, 2027).
All outstanding principal and accrued interest shall be due and payable on the
Maturity Date unless previously converted.

**Interest Rate:** 8% per annum, simple interest, accruing from the date of
issuance. Interest is not payable in cash but converts to equity alongside
principal upon a Qualified Financing or at maturity.

**Valuation Cap:** $50,000,000 pre-money valuation

**Discount Rate:** 25% discount to the price per share in the Qualified
Financing

**Conversion Price:** The lower of (i) the price obtained by dividing the
Valuation Cap by the fully diluted pre-money capitalization at the time of the
Qualified Financing, or (ii) 75% (reflecting the 25% discount) of the price
per share paid by new investors in the Qualified Financing.

---

### CONVERSION EVENTS

**Qualified Financing:** An equity financing of at least $8,000,000 in gross
proceeds (excluding conversion of the Notes and any other convertible
instruments). Upon a Qualified Financing, all outstanding Notes shall
automatically convert into the equity securities sold in such financing at the
Conversion Price.

**Non-Qualified Financing:** If the Company raises equity financing of less
than $8,000,000, the Notes shall convert at the option of each holder (not
automatic) at the Conversion Price. Each holder may elect to convert or to
continue holding the Note until Maturity.

**Change of Control:** Upon a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all
assets before conversion, each holder may elect to either: (i) receive 2.0x the
outstanding principal plus accrued interest in cash, or (ii) convert the Note
into Common Stock at the Conversion Price using only the Valuation Cap (without
discount).

**Maturity:** If Notes have not converted or been repaid prior to the Maturity
Date, each holder may elect to either: (i) demand repayment of principal plus
accrued interest, or (ii) convert at the Valuation Cap price into Seed Preferred
Stock with 1x non-participating liquidation preference and standard terms.

---

### MOST-FAVORED-NATION (MFN) CLAUSE

If the Company issues any convertible note, SAFE, or similar instrument after
the closing of the Notes with terms more favorable to the investors thereof
(including a lower valuation cap, higher discount rate, or additional investor
rights), each Note holder may elect to amend their Note to adopt any or all of
such more favorable terms. This MFN right applies individually and is not an
all-or-nothing election; holders may cherry-pick specific terms.

---

### PRO-RATA RIGHTS

All Note holders (not just Major Investors) shall have the right to participate
pro rata in the Qualified Financing based on their as-converted ownership
percentage at the time of the Qualified Financing. This right extends to the
Qualified Financing only and does not carry forward to subsequent rounds unless
separately negotiated.

---

### ADDITIONAL TERMS

**Subordination:** The Notes shall be subordinate to any senior indebtedness
approved by the Board, including revolving credit facilities up to $2,000,000.

**No Prepayment:** The Company may not prepay the Notes without the consent of
holders of a majority of the outstanding principal.

**Board Rights:** None. Accel shall receive standard information rights (monthly
financial statements and quarterly updates) but no Board seat or observer right
prior to conversion.

**Information Rights:** Monthly unaudited financial statements within 25 days of
month-end. Quarterly management update within 30 days of quarter-end. Annual
operating budget within 30 days of fiscal year start.

**No-Shop:** 45 days from execution of this Term Sheet. Company shall not
solicit or enter into any term sheet or binding agreement for an equity
financing, convertible instrument, or debt facility during this period.

**Key Covenants (pre-conversion):**
- No change of CEO without Accel's consent
- No incurrence of debt exceeding $500,000 (other than the Notes) without
  consent of holders of a majority of outstanding principal
- No issuance of equity securities (other than under the employee incentive plan)
  without consent of a majority of Note holders
- Annual operating expenses shall not exceed 110% of the Board-approved budget
  without prior Board approval

**Expenses:** Company to pay Accel's legal fees up to $20,000, payable at
closing.

**Closing Conditions:**
- Satisfactory completion of confirmatory due diligence
- Execution of Note Purchase Agreement
- Company representations and warranties
- Receipt of legal opinion from Company counsel
