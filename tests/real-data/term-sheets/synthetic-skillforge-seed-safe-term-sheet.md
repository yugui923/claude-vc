### SAFE TERMS

**Conversion:** Each SAFE converts into shares of the Company's Preferred Stock
sold in the next priced Equity Financing (as defined below) at a price per share
equal to the Post-Money Valuation Cap divided by the Company Capitalization
(as defined in the SAFE).

**Equity Financing Threshold:** A bona fide transaction with the principal
purpose of raising capital, in which the Company issues and sells Preferred Stock
at a fixed valuation. No minimum raise threshold — any priced round triggers
conversion.

**Liquidity Event:** If a Change of Control or IPO occurs before conversion,
each SAFE holder receives the greater of (i) the SAFE amount, or (ii) the
amount payable on the number of shares of Common Stock equal to the SAFE amount
divided by the Liquidity Price.

**Dissolution Event:** If the Company dissolves before conversion, each holder
receives its SAFE amount, pari passu with other SAFEs and senior to Common Stock.

### MFN CLAUSE (EXPANDED)

The Company represents that it will not issue any convertible security (including
SAFEs, convertible notes, KISS agreements, or any other instrument convertible
into equity) on terms materially more favorable than those provided in these
SAFEs without offering each SAFE holder the opportunity to amend their SAFE to
incorporate such more favorable terms.

This MFN right explicitly covers:
1. SAFEs with a lower post-money valuation cap
2. SAFEs with a discount on the next round price
3. Convertible notes with a lower valuation cap or higher discount
4. Any convertible instrument with additional investor protections, information
   rights, or governance rights not provided in these SAFEs

Each holder may elect to adopt favorable terms individually and selectively.

### SIDE LETTER — PRIMARY VENTURE PARTNERS

In connection with Primary Venture Partners' $1,500,000 investment, the Company
and PVP have agreed to the following supplemental terms (the "Side Letter"),
which apply solely to PVP:

**Monthly Financial Reporting:** The Company shall deliver to PVP monthly
unaudited financial statements including income statement, balance sheet, and
cash flow statement within 20 days of each month-end, and a written narrative
management update within 25 days of each month-end.

**Board Observer Seat:** PVP shall have the right to designate one
representative to attend all meetings of the Company's Board of Directors in a
non-voting, observer capacity. The observer shall receive all Board materials on
the same timeline as directors. The Company may exclude the observer from
discussions relating to matters in which PVP has a material conflict of interest.

**Consent Right on Debt:** The Company shall not incur, assume, or guarantee any
indebtedness (including equipment financing, lines of credit, venture debt, or
convertible notes, but excluding trade payables in the ordinary course) in
excess of $250,000 in the aggregate outstanding at any time without PVP's prior
written consent.

**Consent Right on Related-Party Transactions:** Any transaction between the
Company and any founder, officer, director, or holder of more than 5% of the
Company's equity requiring payment or transfer of value exceeding $50,000 shall
require PVP's prior written consent.

### PRO-RATA SIDE LETTER — PRIMARY VENTURE PARTNERS

PVP shall have the right to participate in the next Equity Financing (the round
in which the SAFEs convert) in an amount equal to up to 2x its pro-rata
ownership percentage (i.e., up to 20% of the round based on its 10% ownership).
This over-allocation right is personal to PVP and non-transferable.

PVP shall also have standard pro-rata rights in all subsequent equity financings
for as long as it holds at least 5% of the Company on a fully diluted basis.

All other SAFE holders investing $200,000 or more shall receive standard
pro-rata rights (1x) in the next Equity Financing, without over-allocation.
