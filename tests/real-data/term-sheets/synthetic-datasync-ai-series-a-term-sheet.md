# TERM SHEET
## Series A Preferred Stock Financing of DataSync AI, Inc.

**Date:** January 15, 2026
**Confidential**

This Term Sheet summarizes the principal terms of the Series A Preferred Stock
Financing of DataSync AI, Inc. (the "Company"). This term sheet is for discussion
purposes only and is not binding on the parties except for the provisions relating
to confidentiality, exclusivity, and legal fees.

---

### OFFERING TERMS

**Security:** Series A Preferred Stock (the "Series A Preferred")

**Closing Date:** On or before February 28, 2026

**Amount Raised:** $15,000,000

**Pre-Money Valuation:** $60,000,000

**Price Per Share:** $4.80 per share (based on 12,500,000 fully diluted shares
outstanding pre-money)

**Investors:**
| Investor | Amount | Shares |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Acme Ventures (Lead) | $10,000,000 | 2,083,333 |
| TechFund Capital | $3,000,000 | 625,000 |
| Angel Syndicate (various) | $2,000,000 | 416,667 |
| **Total** | **$15,000,000** | **3,125,000** |

**Post-Money Valuation:** $75,000,000

**Capitalization Pre-Financing:**
- Founders' Common Stock: 8,000,000 shares (64%)
- Employee Option Pool (existing): 1,500,000 shares (12%)
- SAFE Holders (converting): 1,800,000 shares (14.4%)
- Unallocated Option Pool: 1,200,000 shares (9.6%)

**Option Pool Expansion:** The Company's option pool shall be increased to 15%
of the post-financing fully diluted capitalization (from the current 9.6%),
with the increase coming from the pre-money valuation.

### CHARTER PROVISIONS

**Dividends:** Non-cumulative dividends of 8% per annum, payable when and if
declared by the Board. Participation with Common Stock on an as-converted basis.

**Liquidation Preference:** 1.0x non-participating preferred. In the event of any
liquidation, dissolution or winding up of the Company, the holders of Series A
Preferred shall be entitled to receive, prior to any distribution to the holders
of Common Stock, an amount equal to the greater of (i) the Original Purchase
Price plus declared but unpaid dividends, or (ii) the amount such holder would
receive on an as-converted basis.

**Conversion:** Each share of Series A Preferred shall be convertible, at the
option of the holder, at any time, into shares of Common Stock. The initial
conversion rate shall be 1:1, subject to customary anti-dilution adjustments.

**Anti-Dilution:** Broad-based weighted average anti-dilution protection.

**Automatic Conversion:** Each share of Series A Preferred shall automatically
convert into Common Stock upon (i) the closing of a firmly underwritten public
offering with gross proceeds of at least $50,000,000 (a "Qualified IPO"), or
(ii) the vote of holders of a majority of the then-outstanding shares of
Series A Preferred.

**Voting Rights:** The Series A Preferred shall vote together with the Common
Stock on an as-converted basis on all matters. Protective provisions requiring
the consent of holders of a majority of the Series A Preferred include:

1. Any amendment to the Certificate of Incorporation that adversely affects
   the Series A Preferred
2. Creation of any new class of shares senior to or on parity with Series A
3. Any merger, sale, or change of control transaction
4. Redemption or repurchase of Common Stock (except for repurchases from
   employees at cost upon termination)
5. Declaration or payment of any dividend
6. Increase or decrease in the authorized number of shares
7. Incurrence of indebtedness exceeding $500,000
8. Any transaction with a founder, officer, or affiliate

---

### INVESTOR RIGHTS

**Board of Directors:** The Board shall consist of five (5) members:
- Two (2) designated by Acme Ventures
- Two (2) designated by the Common Stock holders (initially the CEO and one founder)
- One (1) independent member mutually agreed upon

**Information Rights:** Standard quarterly financial statements, annual audited
financials, and annual operating budget. Monthly financial reports to investors
holding at least 1,000,000 shares.

**Participation Rights (Pro-Rata):** Each Major Investor (holding at least
500,000 shares of Series A Preferred) shall have the right to purchase its
pro rata share of any future issuance of equity securities.

**Registration Rights:** Standard S-3 registration rights, beginning 180 days
after IPO. Two demand registrations. Unlimited piggyback registration rights.
Company to bear all registration expenses (excluding underwriting discounts
and commissions).

---

### ADDITIONAL TERMS

**Right of First Refusal / Co-Sale:** Company and then investors shall have a
right of first refusal on founder shares. Investors shall have co-sale rights
with founders.

**Drag-Along:** Holders of a majority of Common Stock (including as-converted
Preferred), voting together, may require all stockholders to approve a sale of
the Company, provided the transaction values the Company at no less than 3x the
aggregate Original Purchase Price.

**No-Shop / Exclusivity:** The Company agrees not to solicit, initiate, or
encourage any competing financing proposals for a period of 45 days from the
date of this Term Sheet.

**Employee Matters:**
- All employees to sign standard CIIA agreements
- Founder vesting: 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff, single-trigger acceleration
  of 25% upon change of control, double-trigger acceleration of 100%
- Standard 90-day post-termination exercise period for options

**Expenses:** Company to pay reasonable legal fees of lead investor, capped at
$40,000.

**Closing Conditions:**
- Satisfactory completion of due diligence
- Approval of definitive documents
- Key person insurance on CEO ($3,000,000)

---

**NON-STANDARD PROVISIONS (for analysis purposes):**
- Founder single-trigger acceleration of 25% is non-standard (typically only double-trigger)
- 8% non-cumulative dividends (slightly above standard 6%)
- 3x drag-along threshold (standard is typically 2x or simple majority)
- $500K debt cap is restrictive (standard is often $1M+)
- Two board seats for lead investor in a 5-seat board gives significant control
