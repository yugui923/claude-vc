### SAFE TERMS

**Conversion:** Each SAFE converts into shares of the Company's Preferred Stock
upon the next priced Equity Financing at a price per share equal to the Post-
Money Valuation Cap ($8,000,000) divided by the Company Capitalization.

**Equity Financing:** A bona fide transaction in which the Company issues and
sells Preferred Stock at a fixed pre-money valuation. No minimum amount
threshold for triggering conversion.

**Liquidity Event:** Upon a Change of Control or IPO before conversion, each
holder receives the greater of (i) the Purchase Amount, or (ii) the amount
payable on the as-converted Common Stock shares.

**Dissolution Event:** SAFE holders receive their Purchase Amounts, pari passu
with each other and senior to Common Stock.

### ADVISOR SHARES

**Grant:** The Company shall issue 200,000 shares of Common Stock (2.0% of
the pre-SAFE capitalization) to the Company's two advisors as follows:
- Advisor A (Dr. Lena Choi, mapping technology): 120,000 shares
- Advisor B (James Welling, GTM strategy): 80,000 shares

**Vesting:** All 200,000 advisor shares shall vest immediately upon execution
of the advisor agreements. No cliff, no time-based schedule. This is a
departure from the standard 2-year or 4-year advisor vesting and reflects
pre-closing advisory services rendered. The advisor agreements were executed
concurrently with the first SAFE closing.

### KEY-PERSON CLAUSE

If both Co-Founders (Nadia Benamara and Tomasz Kowalski) cease to be active
full-time employees of the Company for any reason (including death, disability,
voluntary departure, or termination for cause) within 36 months of the first
SAFE closing, the holders of at least 60% of the aggregate outstanding SAFE
Purchase Amounts may, by written notice to the Company, require the Company to
undertake one of the following:

1. **Dissolution and distribution of remaining assets** to SAFE holders (senior
   to Common Stock) and then Common Stock holders; or
2. **Appointment of a new CEO** selected by a committee of SAFE holders
   representing at least 50% of outstanding Purchase Amounts, with such CEO
   to have the authority to manage the Company's affairs pending a Board
   determination on the Company's future.

If only one founder departs, no key-person rights are triggered, but the
remaining founder must notify all SAFE holders within 5 business days and
provide a written plan for the Company's continued operations within 30 days.

### AMENDMENT AND WAIVER

Any amendment, modification, or waiver of the SAFEs requires the written consent
of the Company and holders of at least 75% of the aggregate outstanding Purchase
Amounts. This is a supermajority threshold; the standard YC SAFE uses simple
majority of Purchase Amounts.

Notwithstanding the above, the following terms may not be amended without the
unanimous consent of all SAFE holders:
- The Post-Money Valuation Cap
- The Liquidity Event payout priority
- This amendment threshold itself

---

### GEOGRAPHIC RESTRICTION

Each founder agrees that for a period of 5 years from the initial SAFE closing,
the Company shall maintain its primary operations, engineering team, and
headquarters within the San Francisco Bay Area (defined as the counties of
San Francisco, San Mateo, Santa Clara, Alameda, and Contra Costa). Relocating
the Company's headquarters or more than 50% of its engineering team outside the
Bay Area requires the prior written consent of holders of at least 60% of the
aggregate outstanding SAFE Purchase Amounts.

This restriction reflects investor concern regarding talent density, proximity
to key customers, and the Company's competitive positioning in the geospatial
mapping market.

### INFORMATION RIGHTS

**All Investors ($100,000+):** Quarterly written updates (narrative format)
covering business progress, key metrics, financial summary, and material events.
Due within 30 days of quarter-end.

**Lead Investor (Luna Capital):** Monthly financial statements (income
statement, balance sheet, cash position) within 20 days of month-end. Annual
operating budget at least 30 days before each fiscal year. Right to schedule a
30-minute call with each founder once per quarter.

**Investors below $100,000:** Annual written summary only.
