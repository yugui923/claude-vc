### CHARTER PROVISIONS

**Dividends:** Non-cumulative dividends at 6% per annum when and if declared
by the Board. Seed Preferred participates in any Common Stock dividends on an
as-converted basis.

**Liquidation Preference:** 1.0x non-participating preferred. Holders of Seed
Preferred receive the greater of (i) 1.0x Original Purchase Price plus declared
but unpaid dividends, or (ii) the amount receivable on an as-converted basis.
No participation beyond the preference.

**Anti-Dilution:** Broad-based weighted average anti-dilution protection. No
full ratchet. Customary exceptions for employee incentive issuances, strategic
partnerships, and conversion of outstanding notes or SAFEs.

**Conversion:** Each share of Seed Preferred is convertible at any time at the
holder's option into Common Stock at a 1:1 ratio, subject to anti-dilution
adjustments.

**Automatic Conversion:** Seed Preferred converts automatically upon (i) a
Qualified IPO with gross proceeds of at least $30,000,000 at a per-share price
of at least 2x the Original Purchase Price, or (ii) consent of holders of a
majority of the outstanding Seed Preferred.

**Voting Rights:** Seed Preferred votes with Common Stock on all matters on an
as-converted basis. Protective provisions require consent of a majority of
Seed Preferred only for:
1. Sale of all or substantially all Company assets, merger, or dissolution
2. Authorization or issuance of equity senior to Seed Preferred

No other protective provisions apply. Specifically, the following actions do NOT
require Seed Preferred consent: changes in authorized shares, incurrence of debt,
related-party transactions, dividend declarations, or changes in Board size.

### BOARD OF DIRECTORS

**Composition:** The Board shall consist of five (5) members:
- Three (3) designated by the holders of Common Stock (initially Ana Reyes, Dev
  Patel, and one independent member chosen by the founders)
- One (1) designated by a16z crypto
- One (1) mutually agreed independent member

Founders retain clear Board control. Board meetings shall occur at least
quarterly, with a16z receiving standard Board materials at least 5 business
days in advance.

---

### INVESTOR RIGHTS

**Information Rights:** Quarterly unaudited financial statements within 45 days
of quarter-end. Annual unaudited financials within 120 days of fiscal year-end.
Monthly investor updates (narrative, not financial) at Company's discretion.

Only investors holding at least $500,000 in aggregate investment are entitled
to information rights. Investors holding less than $500,000 receive annual
summary updates only.

**Pro-Rata Rights:** Major Investors (holding at least $500,000) have standard
pro-rata rights in future equity financings. No super pro-rata or over-
allocation rights for any investor.

**Registration Rights:** Piggyback registration rights only, beginning 180 days
after IPO. No demand registration rights. Company bears registration expenses
excluding selling commissions.

---

### ADDITIONAL TERMS

**Right of First Refusal / Co-Sale:** Company has ROFR on founder share
transfers. No investor co-sale rights. Founders may transfer up to 10% of
their shares annually without triggering ROFR, provided the transfers are to
family members, trusts, or in secondary sales approved by the Board.

**Drag-Along:** Holders of 70% of Common Stock (on an as-converted basis) may
compel all stockholders to approve a Company sale, provided the per-share
consideration is at least 2x the Seed Preferred Original Purchase Price.

**No-Shop / Exclusivity:** The Company agrees to exclusivity for 30 days from
the date of this Term Sheet. The Company may respond to unsolicited inbound
inquiries but shall not enter into any term sheet or binding commitment.

**Non-Compete:** None. No founder non-compete is required. Standard
non-solicitation of employees and customers for 12 months post-termination
shall apply under the employment agreements.

**Founder Vesting:** Existing founder shares are fully vested. No additional
vesting or re-vesting required at this round.

**Acceleration:** Full double-trigger acceleration for both founders. Upon (i) a
Change of Control followed by (ii) termination without cause or resignation for
good reason within 12 months, 100% of any unvested shares vest immediately.
Single-trigger acceleration is not provided.

**Employee Matters:**
- All employees to sign CIIAs
- 180-day post-termination exercise period for all options (extended from
  standard 90 days)
- Early exercise permitted for all option grants

**Expenses:** Company to pay a16z's legal fees capped at $25,000.

**Closing Conditions:**
- Satisfactory completion of confirmatory due diligence
- Execution of definitive agreements
- Board approval of restated Certificate of Incorporation

**Governing Law:** State of Delaware.
