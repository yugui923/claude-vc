# TERM SHEET
## Series A Preferred Stock Financing of FarmOS Technologies, Inc.

**Date:** April 28, 2025
**Confidential**

This Term Sheet summarizes the principal terms of the proposed Series A Preferred
Stock Financing of FarmOS Technologies, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the
"Company"). Except for "No-Shop," "Confidentiality," "Expenses," and "Exclusive
Partnership," this term sheet is non-binding.

---

### OFFERING TERMS

**Security:** Series A Preferred Stock (the "Series A Preferred")

**Closing Date:** Initial closing on or before June 15, 2025. Second tranche
closing contingent on milestones described below.

**Total Amount:** Up to $10,000,000, in two tranches

**Tranche 1 (at Initial Closing):** $6,000,000
**Tranche 2 (Milestone-Based):** $4,000,000, funded upon achievement of the
Farm Client Milestone (defined below)

**Pre-Money Valuation:** $40,000,000 on a fully diluted basis (for both tranches)

**Price Per Share:** $2.50 per share (based on 16,000,000 fully diluted shares
pre-money)

**Post-Money Valuation (if fully funded):** $50,000,000

**Investors:**
| Investor | Tranche 1 | Tranche 2 | Total | Shares |
|----------|-----------|-----------|-------|--------|
| Syngenta Ventures (Lead) | $4,500,000 | $3,000,000 | $7,500,000 | 3,000,000 |
| Anterra Capital | $1,000,000 | $500,000 | $1,500,000 | 600,000 |
| AgFunder | $500,000 | $500,000 | $1,000,000 | 400,000 |
| **Total** | **$6,000,000** | **$4,000,000** | **$10,000,000** | **4,000,000** |

### MILESTONE-BASED TRANCHE

**Farm Client Milestone:** The Company shall have entered into paid subscription
or license agreements with at least 250 individual farm operations (each
operating a minimum of 100 acres) for the Company's precision agriculture
platform, as verified by an independent audit, within 12 months of the Initial
Closing.

**Milestone Verification:** Within 10 business days of the Company's claim of
milestone achievement, Syngenta Ventures may engage an independent auditor
(at Company expense, up to $25,000) to verify the client count and minimum
acreage thresholds. The auditor's determination shall be final and binding.

**Failure to Achieve Milestone:** If the Farm Client Milestone is not achieved
within 12 months, the Tranche 2 commitment shall expire unless Syngenta
Ventures, in its sole discretion, elects to fund Tranche 2 in whole or in part
at the original terms or at renegotiated terms.

**Anti-Dilution on Tranche 2:** If the Company issues equity at a price below
$2.50 per share between the Initial Closing and the Tranche 2 closing, the
Tranche 2 price per share shall be adjusted downward to the new issuance price
(full ratchet for Tranche 2 only; Tranche 1 has broad-based weighted average).

---

### CHARTER PROVISIONS

**Dividends:** Non-cumulative at 6% per annum, payable when declared by the
Board. Series A participates in Common Stock dividends on an as-converted basis.

**Liquidation Preference:** 1.0x non-participating preferred. Holders receive
the greater of (i) 1.0x Original Purchase Price plus declared unpaid dividends,
or (ii) the as-converted amount.

**Anti-Dilution (Tranche 1):** Broad-based weighted average. Standard carve-outs.

**Conversion:** 1:1 into Common Stock, subject to anti-dilution adjustments.

**Automatic Conversion:** Upon (i) IPO with $50,000,000+ gross proceeds at 3x+
the Original Purchase Price per share, or (ii) consent of 60% of Series A.

**Voting Rights:** Series A votes with Common Stock on an as-converted basis.
Protective provisions require consent of a majority of Series A Preferred for:
1. Charter or Bylaws amendments adversely affecting Series A
2. Senior or pari passu equity issuance
3. Mergers, acquisitions, asset sales, or dissolution
4. Debt exceeding $750,000
5. Related-party transactions
6. Material changes to business scope (including exiting agriculture technology)

---

### STRATEGIC INVESTOR PROVISIONS

**Right of First Refusal on Company Sale:** Syngenta Ventures (or its parent
Syngenta Group) shall have a right of first refusal on any proposed sale,
merger, or change of control of the Company. Upon receipt of a bona fide third-
party offer, the Company shall notify Syngenta Ventures, which shall have 45
days to match the offer on equivalent terms. If Syngenta matches, the Company
must consummate the transaction with Syngenta. This ROFR survives until the
earlier of (i) an IPO, or (ii) the 7th anniversary of the Initial Closing.

**Exclusive Partnership — Corn and Soy:** For a period of 3 years from the
Initial Closing (the "Exclusivity Period"), the Company shall work exclusively
with Syngenta and its affiliates as its commercial partner for the Company's
precision agriculture platform in the corn and soybean segments within the
United States and Canada. During the Exclusivity Period, the Company shall not
enter into distribution, licensing, or co-marketing arrangements with any
Syngenta competitor (including but not limited to Bayer CropScience, Corteva,
and BASF) for corn or soy applications. This exclusivity does not apply to
wheat, rice, specialty crops, or any geography outside the US and Canada.

**Warrant:** Syngenta Ventures shall receive a warrant to purchase additional
shares of Common Stock equal to 2% of the fully diluted capitalization at the
time of exercise, at a strike price based on a $60,000,000 pre-money valuation
($3.00 per share). The warrant is exercisable beginning 18 months after the
Initial Closing and expires on the 5th anniversary of issuance. The warrant
is non-transferable except to Syngenta Group affiliates.

### BOARD OF DIRECTORS

**Composition:** Five (5) members:
- One (1) designated by Syngenta Ventures
- Two (2) designated by Common Stock holders (CEO and one founder)
- One (1) independent director with agriculture industry expertise, mutually
  agreed by Syngenta Ventures and the CEO
- One (1) independent director mutually agreed by all parties

**Board Observer:** Anterra Capital shall have one Board observer seat.

---

### INVESTOR RIGHTS

**Information Rights:** Monthly financial statements and platform usage metrics
(active farms, acreage covered, module adoption) within 20 days of month-end.
Quarterly financial statements within 30 days of quarter-end. Annual audited
financials within 90 days of year-end. Annual operating plan at least 30 days
before each fiscal year.

**Pro-Rata Rights:** Major Investors ($500,000+ in Series A) have standard
pro-rata rights in future equity financings. No super pro-rata rights.

**Registration Rights:** Two demand registrations post-IPO +180 days. Unlimited
piggyback. S-3 when eligible. Company pays expenses except underwriting
discounts.

---

### ADDITIONAL TERMS

**Right of First Refusal / Co-Sale:** Company and then investors have ROFR on
founder shares. Investors have co-sale rights.

**Drag-Along:** 60% of all shares (as-converted), including a majority of
Series A, may compel a Company sale. Subject to Syngenta's ROFR described above.

**No-Shop:** 60 days from the date of this Term Sheet.

**Founder Vesting:** 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff from the Closing Date.
Prior service not credited. Double-trigger acceleration: Change of Control plus
involuntary termination within 12 months results in 100% vesting.

**Key Persons:** Matt Sorensen (CEO) and Dr. Anya Petrov (CTO). If either
departs voluntarily within 24 months of Closing, the Tranche 2 obligation
is void (if not yet funded) and Syngenta Ventures may appoint an interim
replacement pending Board action.

**Employee Matters:**
- All employees sign CIIAs and invention assignment agreements
- Standard 90-day post-termination exercise period
- Hardware-related IP assigned exclusively to the Company

**Expenses:** Company pays Syngenta Ventures' legal fees up to $45,000 and
independent auditor fees for milestone verification up to $25,000.

**Closing Conditions:**
- Completion of legal, financial, technical, and IP due diligence
- Freedom-to-operate opinion for the Company's core hardware/software patents
- Key person insurance of $3,000,000 on CEO and CTO
- Execution of the Exclusive Partnership Agreement as a condition to closing
