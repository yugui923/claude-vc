# TERM SHEET
## Series A Preferred Stock Financing of GreenGrid Energy, Inc.

**Date:** July 14, 2025
**Confidential**

This Term Sheet outlines the principal terms for the proposed Series A Preferred
Stock Financing of GreenGrid Energy, Inc., a Delaware public benefit corporation
(the "Company"). Other than "No-Shop," "Confidentiality," and "Legal Fees,"
this term sheet creates no binding obligations.

---

### CHARTER PROVISIONS

**Dividends:** Cumulative preferred dividend at 4% per annum, accruing daily
and compounding annually. Payable upon a Liquidation Event, redemption, or when
declared by the Board. This below-market rate reflects the Company's
reinvestment-first philosophy and was negotiated as part of BEV's impact terms.

**Liquidation Preference:** 1.0x non-participating preferred. Holders receive
the greater of (i) 1.0x Original Purchase Price plus all accrued and unpaid
dividends, or (ii) the as-converted amount. No participation beyond the
preference.

**Automatic Conversion:** Series A Preferred converts automatically into Common
Stock at the then-effective conversion ratio upon:
- (i) A Qualified IPO with gross proceeds of at least $40,000,000, or
- (ii) Consent of 60% of outstanding Series A Preferred, or
- (iii) Whenever the Fair Market Value of Common Stock (as determined by an
  independent 409A valuation) equals or exceeds 2.0x the Series A Original
  Purchase Price for two consecutive quarters. Note: the 2x threshold is below
  the typical 3x used in most term sheets, enabling earlier automatic conversion.

**Anti-Dilution:** Broad-based weighted average. Standard carve-outs.

**Conversion:** Voluntary conversion at any time at a 1:1 ratio subject to
anti-dilution adjustments.

**Voting Rights:** Series A votes together with Common Stock on an as-converted
basis. Protective provisions requiring consent of a majority of Series A
Preferred:
1. Changes to Charter or Bylaws adversely affecting Series A
2. Issuance of senior or pari passu equity
3. Merger, acquisition, asset sale, or dissolution
4. Incurrence of debt above $1,000,000
5. Related-party transactions with founders or officers
6. Material changes to the Company's public benefit purpose

---

### IMPACT COVENANTS

**B Corp Certification:** The Company shall obtain certified B Corporation
status from B Lab within 18 months of the Closing Date. Failure to obtain or
maintain certification constitutes an Event of Default under the Investor Rights
Agreement, entitling holders of a majority of Series A Preferred to appoint an
additional Board member until certification is achieved.

**Carbon Offset Verification:** Beginning in Q1 2026, the Company shall engage
an independent third-party verifier (approved by BEV from a list including
Verra, Gold Standard, and SCS Global) to audit and certify the Company's
carbon offset claims on an annual basis. Verification reports shall be delivered
to all investors within 30 days of completion.

**ESG Reporting:** The Company shall provide quarterly ESG reports to all
investors, covering: (i) Scope 1, 2, and 3 greenhouse gas emissions, (ii)
renewable energy generation and distribution metrics, (iii) community impact
metrics including jobs created and energy cost savings delivered, and (iv)
diversity metrics across the workforce and leadership. Reports are due within
45 days of each quarter-end.

**Public Benefit Purpose:** The Company's Certificate of Incorporation shall
maintain a specific public benefit purpose of "accelerating the transition to
distributed clean energy." Any amendment to or removal of this purpose requires
the consent of holders of 80% of Series A Preferred.

---

### BOARD OF DIRECTORS

**Composition:** Five (5) members:
- Two (2) designated by Breakthrough Energy Ventures
- Two (2) designated by the holders of Common Stock (initially both founders)
- One (1) independent director with climate-tech expertise, mutually agreed

**ESG Committee:** The Board shall form an ESG Committee within 90 days of
Closing, composed of one BEV designee, one founder, and the independent
director. The committee shall meet quarterly to review impact metrics and
compliance with Impact Covenants.

---

### INVESTOR RIGHTS

**Information Rights:** Monthly financial statements and impact dashboards
within 20 days of month-end. Quarterly financials and ESG reports within 45
days of quarter-end. Annual audited financials within 90 days of year-end.

**Pro-Rata Rights:** Major Investors (holding at least $1,000,000 in Series A
Preferred) have standard pro-rata rights in future rounds.

**Registration Rights:** Two demand registrations (post-IPO +180 days).
Unlimited piggyback rights. S-3 when eligible. Company pays expenses except
underwriting discounts.

---

### ADDITIONAL TERMS

**Right of First Refusal / Co-Sale:** Company and then investors have ROFR on
founder shares. Investors have co-sale rights on founder sales.

**Drag-Along:** 65% of all outstanding shares (as-converted), with BEV consent,
may compel a Company sale, provided the transaction values Series A Preferred
at no less than 1.5x the Original Purchase Price.

**No-Shop:** 45 days from the date of this Term Sheet.

**Founder Vesting:** 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff. Double-trigger
acceleration: Change of Control plus termination within 12 months results in
100% vesting.

**Employee Matters:**
- All employees sign CIIAs
- Standard 90-day post-termination exercise period
- 10b5-1 trading plan required for all officers and directors post-IPO

**Expenses:** Company pays BEV's legal fees up to $50,000.

**Closing Conditions:**
- Completion of due diligence (legal, financial, technical, and environmental)
- Execution of Impact Covenant agreement as an exhibit to the IRA
- Key person insurance of $5,000,000 on each founder
- Board-approved 2025-2026 operating plan
- Confirmation of the Company's Delaware PBC status
